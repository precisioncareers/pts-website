What we can help you with

Vigorous Recruitment campaign

Our team works around the clock to find your Key Personnel. We assign a team of Recruiters and Sourcers led by an Account Executive to handle your mandate efficiently. With our global footprint, we work around the clock through the weekend to find suitable candidates.

In-Depth Compliance Checks

PTS works diligently to screen individuals we source. We run each candidate through a rigorous compliance check to ensure they meet your needs. An Account Executive will thoroughly vet candidates before presenting them to our clients.

Managing Letter of Commitment

Once our team finds your Key Personnel, we'll attain a LOC to attach to your proposal. While awaiting the award, our team will keep candidates warm. If a candidate drops out of the process at any point, we will work diligently to replace the individual.
