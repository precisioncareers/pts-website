Candidates

PTS is disrupting the hyper-transactional nature of recruitment in the government contracting sector by building long-term relationships with our candidates. Our approach is to treat our candidates like customers. We provide proactive updates on recruitment status and personality assessment feedback. PTS team members maintain high-touch engagement with our candidates throughout the recruitment process. We share regular industry updates and job openings with current and former candidates as we seek to be your career partners. We realize that we are not only representing the PTS brand but also an extension of our customers' brands to the industry talent base.

We are with you for your career not just a job!

Complete Profile Assessment

We'll help you create an in-depth profile outlining your skill set, personality assessment, work experience, and educational qualifications. Our talent acquisition professionals can answer your profile or job-specific questions.

Streamlined Application Process

Our streamlined application process ensures you waste no time applying for a new career. We've created a customized approach designed specifically for government contracting. Our process makes it easy to use for your next position.

Lifelong Career Partners

Working with PTS will increase your likelihood of getting hired. Our team of Account Executives, recruiters, and Sourcers understand the importance of finding the right fit for candidates and customers. We work diligently to pair both parties in a mutually beneficial relationship.
