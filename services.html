Our Services

Retained C-Suite Search

PTS provides a fully retained, highly consultative search to bring top industry talent to our clients. We partner closely with executive stakeholders for C-Suite roles to understand the positions' rationale.

Contained Executive Search

The core of any business is hiring and retaining highly experienced management teams. Our unique technology approach provides our customers with an in-depth analysis of each candidate we present, ensuring an optimal hiring process.

Technical/Cleared Talent

We specialize in the "purple unicorns" technical roles such as IT, Shared Services, Medical, and Classified positions. From NATO Secret to TS/SCI Full Poly, we deeply understand the nuances of penetrating this niche talent base.

Bid & Proposal Support

PTS bolts onto your Capture teams to find the most compliant and compelling talent for your bids. We lead an intense recruitment campaign, conduct compliance checks, and manage the Letter of Commitment process.

Diversity & Veteran Recruiting

The PTS team is highly diverse, with thirty-five members across eleven countries. We're a Veteran Owned Small Business working closely with American Corporate Partners and other veterans' groups to help service members transition.
