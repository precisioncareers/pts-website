Why PTS?

Precision Talent Solutions is not your average executive search firm or recruitment agency. We are consulting partners helping our customers solve the greatest challenge of the government contracting industry: talent.

Over 400 years of cumulative government contracting experience from the C-Suite level to the field technical professional level, gives our team the knowledge about the programs and agencies driving our industry. We have mapped the industry, and each of our 35+ team members bring their extensive network to our customers.

PTS leverages disruptive technology for recruitment, providing key data points for predictive hiring decisions through video interviews, assessments, and technical scorecards. We invest heavily in sourcing technology, including numerous LinkedIn Recruiter seats and access to other job platforms.

We start every team meeting with the phrase, "treat your candidates like customers," which reflects our philosophy towards empathetic recruitment. We believe in personifying our core values of integrity, communication, respect, initiative, and added value through our recruitment process, and we strive to create a positive experience for both our clients and candidates.
