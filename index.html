Why PTS?

Precision Talent Solutions is not your average executive search firm or recruitment agency. We are consulting partners helping our customers solve the greatest challenge of the government contracting industry: talent.

Experience

Over 400 years of cumulative government contracting experience from the C-Suite level to the field technical professional level, gives our team the knowledge about the programs and agencies driving our industry. We have mapped the industry, and each of our 35+ team members bring their extensive network to our customers.

Technology

PTS has revolutionized recruitment by leveraging disruptive technology to deliver the talent that is right for you. Video interviews, personality assessments, leadership success predictor, cognitive assessment, functional fit, 9-block predictor, and technical scorecards, all deliver you key data points to make a predicative hiring decision. We invest heavily in our sourcing technology, including numerous LinkedIn Recruiter seats, and access to Clearance Jobs, Indeed, and X-Ray.

Philosophy

We understand the importance of building the right teams in this industry. Most members of PTS served as hiring managers and understand what you are faced with. We start every team meeting with the phrase, “treat your candidates like customers.” Our philosophy towards empathetic recruitment personifies our core values of integrity, communication, respect, initiative, and added value.

PTS delivers government contracting talent acquisition services for Defense, Diplomacy, Aid & Development, and Tech IC. Our team of industry professionals holds extensive experience in operations and management from each of these sectors.

When you work with PTS, you're deploying a group of subject matter experts with expansive networks. PTS team members have the distinct advantage of previously serving in most of the roles we fill.

Top talent

At Precision Talent Solutions, we offer a unique solution to your talent needs. Our technology and experience allow us to find the best candidates, helping you save time and money.

PTS is a member of the International Stability Operations Association (ISOA), and our President, Jake Frazer, serves on the Board of Directors.

Our company is Veteran-founded and owned. We value those who served our country and its allies. Roughly one-third of our employees served in the U.S. or European armed services.

PTS is part of an elite group of entrepreneurially-inspired companies with strong ties to technology. In 2022, we ranked 4,675 among the fastest-growing companies in America.
