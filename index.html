Why PTS?

PTS is not your average Exec Search firm or recruitment agency. We are consultative partners helping our customers solve the most significant challenge of the industry, talent.

Experience

With over 400 years of cumulative government contracting experience from C-Suite to the field, our team knows the programs and agencies driving our industry. We have mapped the competitors, and each of our 30+ team members bring their network to our customers.

Technology

PTS is revolutionizing recruitment by leveraging disruptive technology to include video interviews and personality assessments with a chance of leadership success predictor, cognitive assessment, functional fit, 9-block predictor, and technical scorecards. Our virtual assessment center increases hiring quality and cuts overall costs. We invest heavily in our sourcing technology, including dozens of subscriptions to LinkedIn Recruiter, Clearance Jobs, Indeed, and X-Ray.

Philosophy

Our recruiters understand the importance of building the right teams in this industry, as many of us served as hiring managers before PTS. We start every team meeting with the phrase “treat your candidates like customers”. Our philosophy towards empathetic recruitment personifies our core values of integrity, communication, respect, initiative, and added value.

PTS delivers talent acquisition services for Defense, Diplomacy, Aid & Development, and Tech IC in government contracting. Our team of industry professionals holds extensive operations and management experience.

When you work with PTS, you're deploying a group of subject matter experts with expansive networks. PTS team members have the distinct advantage of previously serving in most of the roles we fill.
